CIS is a well established, reputed, co-educational, international school situated at Kolkata serving both local and expatriate communities. Link on advantageous location: CIS is advantageously located at 45 min from airport and Salt Lake and only 10 min from Peerless Hospital and only 2 min from Ruby General Hospital. CIS prides itself on its warmth and welcoming nature; cosmopolitan culture; open attitude towards students, teachers and parents; safe & secure environment; well trained teachers; modern infrastructure; small class strengths; low student – teacher ratio and global curriculum. CIS believes that the primary purpose of a school is to guide the child in the discovery of themselves and their world…. to identify and nurture their talents.

Just as every seed contains the future tree, each child is born with infinite potential. CIS sees children as seeds to be nurtured – here the teacher is a gardener who helps to bring out the potential already present in the child. CIS is the best possible environment for children to grow, to blossom and to evolve. To help the teachers and the management do an effective job in Calcutta International School Society, it is run by a Board of Governors who are elected from the parents of current students of the school. They value add in making policy decisions as well as help in the implementation and review processes.
